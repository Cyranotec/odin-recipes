Blue Cheese and Pear Tartlets

So beautiful and delicious

These tasty pear and blue cheese tartlets take little time to prepare but will impress your guests!

Ingredients

  • 1 ripe pear - peeled, cored, and chopped
  • 4 ounces blue cheese, crumbled
  • 2 tablespoons light cream ground black pepper to taste
  • 1 (1.9 ounce) package mini phyllo tart shells

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Mix together pear, blue cheese, and cream in a baking dish. Season with pepper. Spoon mixture into phyllo shells.
  3. Bake in the preheated oven for 15 minutes. Serve warm.
